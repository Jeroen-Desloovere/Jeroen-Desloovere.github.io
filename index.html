Frequently Asked

We want everyone to feel comfortable and like themselves, so please wear whatever makes you happy! Most of the celebration will be outdoors on grass, so we recommend keeping that in mind when choosing your outfit and shoes.

Agroturizëm Gjepali is located just outside the city, with plenty of space and easy parking available for anyone planning to drive. Please note that the address can point navigation apps to a crossroads rather than the venue itself. When you reach the crossroads, turn into the side road and continue until the end. The exact coordinates of the venue are 41°19'40"N 19°33'41"E.

If you'd rather relax and fully enjoy the celebration without worrying about transportation, simply share where you'll be staying and we'll be happy to help coordinate transport for you.

Albania uses the Albanian Lek (ALL). Euros are accepted in many tourist-facing places, but Lek is preferred and usually simpler. Cards are common in hotels and larger restaurants in Durrës and Tirana, while smaller places may prefer cash.

If the weather allows it, the entire event will be held outdoors. In September, evenings are usually warm, but we recommend bringing a light layer.

Citizens of EU countries, the UK, the US, Canada, Australia, and many others do not require a visa for short stays in Albania. We recommend checking your country's specific requirements with a reputable resource.
