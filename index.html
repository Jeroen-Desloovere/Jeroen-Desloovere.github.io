Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked

Our wedding will be an adult-only celebration, allowing everyone to relax and enjoy the evening fully. We kindly ask that children are not brought to the ceremony or reception. We appreciate your understanding and hope this gives you a wonderful night off!

We'd love for our guests to dress in garden-elegant / cocktail attire. Soft colours — ivory, blush, sage, warm earth tones — feel beautiful in the Mediterranean evening light. Please avoid white or black. As the celebration is outdoors, we recommend comfortable heels or wedges rather than stilettos, and consider bringing a light wrap for the later hours.

Agroturizëm Gjepali is outside the city, so we recommend using the coordinated guest transfers, arranging a taxi/driver, or driving only if you have a designated driver. If you plan to come by car, please tell us in your RSVP so we can coordinate parking and arrival details.

Albania uses the Albanian Lek (ALL). Euros are accepted in many tourist-facing places, but Lek is preferred and usually simpler. Cards are common in hotels and larger restaurants in Durrës and Tirana, while smaller places may prefer cash.

Yes — the ceremony and dinner are planned in the outdoor garden/countryside setting at Agroturizëm Gjepali. In September, evenings are usually warm, but we recommend bringing a light layer. We'll keep you updated if the weather forecast changes.

We'd love for our ceremony to be an unplugged experience — phones and cameras away so everyone can be fully present. Our photographer will capture every beautiful moment. After the ceremony, please feel free to take as many photos as you like! We'll share a gallery of professional images with all guests after the wedding.

Citizens of EU countries, the UK, the US, Canada, Australia, and many others do not require a visa for short stays in Albania. We recommend checking your country's specific requirements with the Albanian embassy or a reputable travel resource. Visit our travel page for more practical guidance.

Your presence truly is our greatest gift. If you'd like to contribute something, we have a small honeymoon fund — details enclosed with your invitation. Please do not feel any obligation; sharing this day with the people we love is more than enough.
