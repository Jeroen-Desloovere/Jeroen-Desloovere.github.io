Where to Stay

A practical guide to where to stay near Agroturizëm Gjepali — from the closest countryside options to Durrës seafront and Tirana city stays.

Booking early is important. The venue is in the Gjepalaj/Shijak countryside, so the best place to stay depends on whether you prefer being closest to the wedding, by the sea in Durrës, or in the city in Tirana. Please share your accommodation in the RSVP so we can coordinate transport.

Helpful Advice

Booking Tips

Book Early

September can still be busy, especially in popular coastal areas and in Tirana. Book once your travel dates are clear, and prioritise options with straightforward cancellation terms.

Share Your Location

Please add your hotel or apartment address in the RSVP when you know it. This helps us plan guest transport and pickup points more realistically.

Plan the Return

The venue is rural, so do not rely on walking or finding last-minute transport late at night. Use the coordinated transfer or arrange a trusted driver in advance.
